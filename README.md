# Portfolio Website Customization Instructions

Welcome to the portfolio website repository! Follow these instructions to customize your portfolio and add games.

## Customizing Your Portfolio
1. **Edit the Content**: Open the `index.html` file and modify the sections containing your details, such as your name, bio, and contact information.
2. **Change the Colors**: Navigate to the `styles.css` file. Look for color properties and adjust them according to your preference.
3. **Update Images**: Replace the default images in the `images` folder with your desired images. Ensure they are named correctly to reflect in your portfolio.

## Adding Games
1. **Locate Game Section**: In the `index.html`, find the section designated for games.
2. **Add Your Game**: Create a new `<div>` for your game, using the structure of existing games as a template.
3. **Link the Game**: Add a link or embed code to showcase your game. Ensure the paths are correct for assets used.

## Preview Changes
After making changes, open your `index.html` in a browser to preview the updates. Don't forget to test for responsiveness!

## Commit Your Changes
Make sure to commit your changes to reflect them on your live website!
